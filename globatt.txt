PEW RESEARCH CENTER - Global Attitudes & Trends

Spring 2014 44-Nation Survey

It is important to read this information before using the Pew Research Center's Spring 2014 Global Attitudes survey dataset. 

 
1.	General population weight:
	
	The weight that must be used when analyzing the attitudes and opinions of the general populations are:
		
	General population variable name:  Weight
			
	Weight by weight for this analysis. 


2.	The full topline results are for the general populations, unless otherwise stated. To access topline trends, please use the Pew Global Attitudes & Trends Question Database on our website: http://www.pewglobal.org/question-search/     


3.	The topline results exclude data for questions where there are fewer than 100 observations for a particular country. This data is, however, included in the full dataset. 


4.	The sample in Ukraine includes all areas available for surveying in 2014, including the oblasts of Luhans'k, Donets'k, as well as Crimea.  


5.	The questionnaire includes full question wording as well as answer category values.


6.	Methodological overviews of each survey country, including sample design, mode, languages, fieldwork dates, sample size, margin of error, and representativeness are available on our methods website: http://www.pewglobal.org/international-survey-methodology/?year_select=2014


7.	Certain variables have been eliminated from the dataset either because of disclosure sensitivities or because of administrative errors.
